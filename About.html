Snow Dreams

Our Mission

Our website aims to provide a place for snowboarders and ski enthusiasts alike to find the way to their next adventure. We know how difficult it is to find a cool new spot in this niche and that's why we're here to help.

Our Story

Founded by Lucas Snow in 2025, we are a group of passionate snow lovers who found that finding the next spot to conquer was the most difficult part of the hobby. We simply lacked a streamlined place to sort out our adventures. This is why Lucas came to us with his idea, and the rest is history.
